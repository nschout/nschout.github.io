Chocolate-Covered Strawberries Recipe

This recipe requires a microwave, a small container (at least 16oz) to melt chocolate in, NON-STICK parchment paper, and a dish to set the Chocolate-Covered Strawberries to cool.

Serves 10 people with a serving size of 2 strawberries.

Ingredients

  • 20 large strawberries (With leaves or stems attached)
  • 16oz Semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 Tablespoon vegetable shortening

Directions

  1. Wash and dry the strawberries.
  2. Place a layer of non-stick parchment paper over the dish you will be using to cool the strawberries on.
  3. Put the chocolate and vegetable shortening in a small container.
  4. Microwave the chocolate and shortening for 2 minutes.
  5. Mix the chocolate and shortening until it is smooth.
  6. Dip each of the strawberries into the chocolate mixture by the leaves.
  7. Lay the dipped strawberries onto a dish to cool off and harden.
  8. Optional: The dipped strawberries can be placed in a refrigerator for a few minutes to quicken the hardening of the chocolate.
  9. Once they have fully hardened, they can be served.
